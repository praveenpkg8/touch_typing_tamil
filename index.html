Tamil99 Typing Tutor — Free Tamil Touch-Typing Practice

Tamil99 Typing Tutor is a free, browser-based tool for learning touch typing on the Tamil99 keyboard layout — the standard Tamil keyboard layout defined by the Government of Tamil Nadu and adopted by ISO/BIS. It offers 21 structured lessons that begin with home-row consonants and vowels and progress through pulli, gemination, soft-hard pairs, words, and full Tamil sentences, finishing with speed and accuracy challenges. The app requires no account or sign-in, stores all progress locally on the device using IndexedDB, and collects no data. A recommendation engine tracks per-grapheme accuracy across sessions and generates targeted spaced-repetition drills for characters the learner struggles with. A visual keyboard shows finger-zone colour coding and predicts the next required key. Custom practice mode accepts any pasted Tamil text. Suitable for beginners, students preparing for typing exams, Tamil writers, and anyone who wants to learn the Tamil99 keyboard layout. Free to use at touchtypingtamil.netlify.app.

Lesson topics

  • L01–L02 — Home row consonants (mei) and vowels (uyir)
  • L03–L07 — Uyirmei combinations, top row, long vowels, full character matrix
  • L08–L13 — Top/bottom row consonants, pulli, gemination, soft-hard pairs
  • L14–L19 — Words, family terms, numbers, greetings, simple and complex sentences
  • L20–L21 — Speed practice and accuracy challenge
